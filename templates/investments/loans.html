Loan Terms & Conditions
1. Interest Rates and Fees

Tiered Interest Rates:

  • $100 - $10,000: 4.5% APR
  • $10,001 - $50,000: 4.0% APR
  • $50,001 - $100,000: 3.5% APR

Interest is calculated on a simple interest basis. No hidden fees or origination charges apply.

2. Collateral Requirements

Unsecured Loans: Loans up to $25,000 require no collateral.

Secured Loans: Loans above $25,000 may require your investment portfolio as collateral. The collateral value must be at least 120% of the loan amount.

3. Repayment Terms
  • Repayment periods: 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days
  • Payments can be made monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly
  • Automatic payments can be set up from your Elite Wallet
  • Minimum payment is $50 per transaction
4. Early Repayment Benefits

No Prepayment Penalties: You can repay your loan early without any additional fees.

Interest Savings: Early repayment reduces the total interest you pay, as interest is calculated on the outstanding balance.

Credit Score Boost: Early loan repayment may positively impact your credit score for future applications.

5. Late Payment Penalties
  • Grace Period: 5-day grace period after due date
  • Late Fee: $25 or 5% of the missed payment (whichever is greater)
  • Penalty Interest: Additional 2% APR on overdue amounts
  • Default: Loans unpaid for 90+ days may be sent to collections
6. Approval Process

Application Review: Most applications are reviewed within 24 hours

Approval Criteria:

  • Minimum account age: 30 days
  • Minimum credit score: 650 (or equivalent)
  • Debt-to-income ratio below 40%
  • No defaults in the past 12 months

Fund Disbursement: Approved funds are deposited within 48 hours

7. Borrower Rights & Responsibilities

Your Rights:

  • Right to clear loan terms disclosure
  • Right to prepay without penalty
  • Right to dispute charges
  • Right to payment plan modifications in case of hardship

Your Responsibilities:

  • Provide accurate information in your application
  • Make timely payments as per the schedule
  • Notify us immediately of financial hardship
  • Maintain collateral value (for secured loans)
8. Default and Collections

If a loan remains unpaid for 90 days or more, it may be declared in default. In such cases:

  • The full outstanding balance becomes immediately due
  • Your credit score will be negatively impacted
  • The loan may be referred to a collections agency
  • Legal action may be taken to recover the debt
  • Collateral (if applicable) may be liquidated
9. Agreement

By submitting a loan application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these terms and conditions. You also consent to Elite Wealth Capital verifying your financial information and credit history.
